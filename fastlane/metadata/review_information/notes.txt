BoatNav is a navigation app for Dutch inland waterways. No login required.

Test hazard reporting: tap the purple hazard button (left side, above map).
Test location sharing: open Friends panel via the map buttons on the right.
Test in-app purchase: Settings → Upgrade to Pro. Use sandbox account.
Waterway data is fetched from PDOK (Dutch government open data).

The app uses background location to maintain GPS speed and navigation while the user is on the water with the screen off.
