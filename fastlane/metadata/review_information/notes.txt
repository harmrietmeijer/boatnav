BoatNav is a navigation app for Dutch inland waterways. No login required. iPhone only.

BACKGROUND LOCATION — how to test:
1. Tap the navigation button (diamond icon, right side of map)
2. The Navigation panel opens. Tap "Kies startlocatie" → select "Huidige locatie"
3. Tap "Kies bestemming" → "Zoek locatie" → search for "Dordrecht" → select a result
4. Tap "Bereken route" (requires Pro — use sandbox purchase or tap the sailboat icon in Boat Profile 5 times quickly to activate Pro bypass)
5. Once a route is calculated and navigation starts, the app uses background location to continuously track the user's GPS position, update speed readings (km/h and knots), and provide turn-by-turn navigation — even when the screen is off or the app is in the background.

Without background location, GPS speed would freeze and navigation instructions would stop updating when the user locks their phone while on the water.

HAZARD REPORTING: tap the purple button (left side, above map) to report obstacles.
LOCATION SHARING: open Friends panel via map buttons on the right.
